About

Kadamba Independent PU College is a well-regarded educational institution located in Krishnarajapete (K.R. Pet), Mandya district, Karnataka. It is known for its focus on pre-university education and holistic student development. The college was established in 2018 and is situated on Main Road, Krishnarajapete, near the local police station. The institution follows the State Board curriculum prescribed by the Department of Pre-University Education, Karnataka. The institution is noted for having a dedicated faculty that fosters academic excellence and supports student growth. It is described as providing a supportive environment that balances theoretical knowledge with practical application. The college has a dedicated mobile app ("KADAMBA INDEPENDENT PU COLLEGE") to keep parents informed about child activities, attendance, and exam results. The campus includes essential amenities such as functional washrooms and a pucca boundary wall for safety. The curriculum is designed to prepare students for higher education and future career opportunities.
